How would you like to pay?

You can spread your payments over 6 months to 25 years. If you choose to pay the balance within 12 months you will pay no extra costs.

If you split the payments over more than 12 months, there will be an additional 'instalment charge' (like interest), meaning that the total you pay will end up more than we've quoted.
